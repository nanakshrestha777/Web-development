Benefits of Neural Network.



Adaptability

Neural networks can learn and adapt to new data, allowing them to improve their performance over time.



Versatility

Neural networks can be used for a wide range of tasks, including classification, regression, and clustering.



Robustness

Neural networks are resistant to noise and can handle missing or corrupted data, making them robust and reliable.



Scalability

Neural networks can be trained on large datasets and can be deployed to run on distributed systems, allowing them to scale to handle large amounts of data.



Human-like performance

Neural networks can achieve human-like performance on many tasks, such as image and speech recognition.
